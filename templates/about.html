About Rosemary Cakes

Spreading sweetness since 2010 🧁

Who We Are

At Rosemary Cakes, we pour love into every recipe. Based in Kolkata, our bakery started as a humble kitchen dream and quickly became a community favorite for delightful treats and celebration cakes.

Our cakes are baked fresh daily, using the finest ingredients — ensuring flavor, texture, and happiness in every bite.

Our Vision

We believe in celebrating life’s sweetest moments — whether it's a birthday, a wedding, or a simple thank-you cake.

Our vision is to become your go-to bakery for every occasion, combining tradition with innovation, and always putting joy on your plate.

🧁 Creativity
